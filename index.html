About Me

Hi, I'm Idil Ozlem Koc. I am currently a visiting professor at Cal State University Fullerton in the Mihaylo College of Business and Finance. I am also a Professor of Finance at Marmara University School of Banking and Insurance. My research interests are in portfolio management, corporate governance and behavioral finance. Behavioral Finance Study

As part of our research into the trading behaviours of professionals in the finance, business and banking sectors we are conducting an anonymous survey. The goal of this study is to determine if and to what extent finance professionals deviate from rational decision making due to behavioral biases and heuristics when making personal investment decisions. If you are a working professional in the finance, business, banking or investment sectors we are very interested to hear from you!
